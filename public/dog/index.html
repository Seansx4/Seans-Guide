Grooming

Information

The dog Grooming Parlour Wexford is located at Kosy Kennels. Our boarding kennels has a full time grooming parlour on site. Some of our customers call us the Canine Beauticians or even the Pooch Parlour! But we are mainly known as Wexford's Top Dog Groomers!

Helen is our full time, qualified groomer and is committed to providing excellent service and professional grooming. “As your groomer, I pride myself in providing my clients with a safe, clean and warm atmosphere while they are being professionally groomed. With this in mind, my clients' guardians can go about their daily business knowing that their pets are being well cared for.”

Services Includes

  • Styling to breed standard
  • Clipping and Triming
  • Conditioning with luxury conditioners
  • Shampooing and Styling, by a professional
  • Grooming
  • Bathing
  • Gorgeous doggy Perfumes
  • Skin conditions treated
  • Nail clipping only if required
  • Small breeds - Yorkies, Westies, Shih tzu, Scotties, Cairns, Pekingese, etc
  • Large breeds - Spaniels, Collies, Labs, Retrievers, Boxers, etc Giant breeds - Newfoundlands, St Bernards, etc.

Each Dog and Cat enjoys plenty of attention and interaction and our Kennels and Cattery were built to provide the perfect setting for your dog or cat to enjoy their holiday!

When you're picking up your dog from the Groomers just think what a day they've had!

  • Your hairdresser doesn't give you a manicure, pedicure, and clean out your ears.
  • Your hairdresser only clips your head.
  • Your hairdresser doesn't have to de-mat your hair.
  • You don't wiggle, jump and shake while being worked on with sharp paw.
  • Anal glands. This alone should be reason enough. Although most groomers are probably used to it, it's never a pleasant experience to express anal glands. Never!
  • Dog bites. Most dogs usually aren't biters, but there's always a chance that even the sweetest dog can turn around and bite their groomer.
  • Blow Drying. Depending on the size of the dog and coat type, this can take anywhere between 10 minutes to an hour and a half.

And that's just the start !!!!!

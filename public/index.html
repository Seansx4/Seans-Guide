About Kosy Kennels

Welcome to Kosy Kennels

We have been providing dog boarding in Wexford since 1994 and due to demand opened our dedicated boarding cattery in 2007. We are situated just outside Wexford town and close to Rosslare Port in the wonderfully quite area of Murrintown.

As a full time boarding kennel and cattery we find that it is important to look after the mental as well as the physical side of your pet's well-being. Lots of attention and kindness, exercise and play, heated comfortable accommodation and plenty of kind words all in a stress free environment but whatever it takes, we do it!

Our Services Include

  • Kosy Dog Kennels
  • Dedicated Cattery
  • Collection and delivery for both cats and dogs
  • Full time grooming parlour
  • Day Care

Each Dog and Cat enjoys plenty of attention and interaction and our Kennels and Cattery were built to provide the perfect setting for your dog or cat to enjoy their holiday!

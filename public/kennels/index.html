Boarding Kennels

Information

Our dog boarding kennels were first built in 1994. Our Kennels were built so that each of our dog kennels are provided with an individual sleeping and feeding enclosure. Each Kennel is cleaned and disinfected daily. There is access to water at all times and food is prepared and given as per owner's instructions.We also offer a freezer facility for fresh and cooked meats.

We supply high quality dry and canned food. If the dog is on prescription or specialised food eg. Science Plan, Royal Canin, the owner should supply this.

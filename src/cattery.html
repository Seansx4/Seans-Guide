Cattery

Information

Comfy Cattery consists of spacious units. The sleeping quarters can take two to three cats very comfortably. The unit provides heated, Kosy accommodation and allows for privacy. It is very hygienic as it separates the sleeping and litter areas.

Each cat's unit has its own individual exercise run for your pet. The outlook for our guests is interesting; the view for your cat from the cattery is full of wildlife. There is excellent airflow, light and ventilation which is necessary for hygiene. The cattery is designed to allow us to personally inspect and engage with our V.I.C.'s (Very Important Cats!) throughout the day.

Our Cattery supplys bedding, scratching posts, toy's etc, but you are always welcome to bring any of your cat's personal things to the cattery! We stock Whiskas, Go-Cat, Felix and of course treat's.

Take the stress out of travel. Enjoy your holiday knowing that your Cat is in a comfortable, heated, secure Cattery!
